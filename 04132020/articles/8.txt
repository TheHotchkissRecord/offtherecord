Of the thousands upon thousands of lessons history has given us, undoubtedly the most important are as follows: Never get involved in a land war in Asia, and never go against a Sicilian when death is on the line. However, were we to add a third to that illustrious list, it would be the following: Never underestimate mankind’s capacity for unfathomable, almost inspiring stupidity. In that vein, here are just three hilariously painful and painfully hilarious examples of humanity’s unadulterated idiocy.
