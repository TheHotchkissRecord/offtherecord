Announcing The CXXII Editorial Board of The Record
Tuesday, February 4, 2020
-
Lakeville, CT
Dear Members of the Hotchkiss Community,

The time has come to announce the CXXII Editorial Board of The Hotchkiss Record. The current executive board and faculty advisors are extremely proud of all the work done by these individuals and are confident in their ability to continue to make The Record the best newspaper produced by Hotchkiss students. Please congratulate the new editors when you see them.
While the official changeover date for the staff is still not for a couple of weeks, training for some editors has already begun. We hope you enjoy the final issues under the CXXI board.

Nicholas Lorentzen,
On Behalf of The CXXI Executive Editorial Board of The Record
