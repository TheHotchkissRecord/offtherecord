Content preview: 
Is “Black Lives Matter” a partisan slogan instead of one that addresses moral issues? Is “the white officer shot the black man” more or less objective than political euphemisms like “an officer-involved shooting?” Journalists struggle to present plain facts about the news without taking sides when it comes to moral issues. For far too long, journalism has failed to fulfill its duty to provide information and to not only be factual, but also fair. It is time for us to rethink the value of objectivity.
